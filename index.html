MEDELA

A SPOTIFY PLAYLIST GENERATOR


Medela is an application that allows users to generate a new ordered spotify playlist that takes into account the danceability, energy and valence of songs.

ABOUT

MORE ABOUT OUR PRODUCT


Stress due to school, anxiety, isolation, feeling mentally under-stimulated due to a lack of human interaction/quarantine forms a global mental health pandemic. Over 970 million people across the world currently suffer from a mental health illness. We have created Medela to help enable and allow the general public to automatically generate a playlist to help them wind down after a long day.
